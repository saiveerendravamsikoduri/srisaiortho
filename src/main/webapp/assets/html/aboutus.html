Sri Sai Tejaswi Hospitals

  • Sri Sai Tejaswi has been a center of medical excellence in the city of Mandapeta .
  • Constantly adapting and growing with the latest medical advancements has been Sri Sai Tejaswi Hospitals forte.
  • With a wide range of departments, services and treatments, the state of the art infrastructure accentuates the hospital’s capabilities in providing cutting edge medical care all the while keeping patient centricity and comfort paramount.

The group of extensively qualified and successful doctors at Sri Sai Tejeswi hospitals ensure the fame associated with the name carries on for generations to come. The skilful and exceptionally trained staff and administration help the smooth and hindrance free functioning of the hospital.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional and compassionate healthcare services to all individuals, regardless of their background or circumstances. We are dedicated to promoting the well-being of our patients by delivering personalized, innovative, and comprehensive medical care.

Vision

We envision a healthier and happier community where everyone has access to exceptional healthcare services, regardless of their background or circumstances. We strive to be a beacon of hope, a center of excellence, and a catalyst for positive change in the lives of the people we serve.

Values

We collaborate effectively, recognizing the value of each team member's contribution. By working together, we achieve more and provide seamless, coordinated care to our patients. We support and inspire one another, fostering a positive and inclusive work environment.
