Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a healthcare profession that helps people recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve their overall physical well-being.Musculoskeletal, Cardio-Respiratory (sometimes also referred to Cardio-Pulmonary) and Neurology. Musculoskeletal – This is the area that deals with injuries related to the muscles, bones and joints of the human muscle and skeletal system.reduce pain, improve joint mobility, increase strength, and improve cardio-respiratory function.
